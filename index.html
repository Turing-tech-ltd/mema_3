Meet Our Principal, President

Blerim Mema

As the Principal and Founder of Mema Accounting Solutions, Blerim holds a Bachelor’s degree in Accounting from Kennesaw State University and a Master’s degree in Accountancy from the Emporia State University. Blerim has managed the accounting of over 20,000 multifamily units. His accountability, flexibility, and dedication to his clients and work make him an excellent asset. He has had over 10 years of experience with the real estate industry, with some of this experience in public accounting where he worked on auditing affordable housing properties.

Coming all the way to the Atlanta area from the country of Albania, Blerim has worked diligently learning a new language and schooling to become the accountant he is today. He enjoys getting outside and spending time with his family. Blerim’s love and passion for the sport of soccer is revealed through his coaching, refereeing, and athleticism when playing soccer himself.
